About Sport Tech Learn

We design active, teacher-ready learning experiences that connect sport, digital thinking and reflection.

What guides us

  • Learning through play that moves from game → strategy → reflection.
  • Inquiry and computational thinking in everyday language, not jargon.
  • Clear links to ACARA (HPE, Digital Technologies, Mathematics & Science).
  • Inclusive by design: mixed-ability classes, multicultural cohorts, strong pathways for girls.
  • Low equipment and minimal-tech options so any school can run it.

Our Pedagogy

Play → Think → Reflect

Students experience the game, name the strategy together, then use a quick prompt or checklist to plan the next round. Rotating roles (player/coach/umpire) build voice, leadership and wellbeing.

Computational Thinking in Sport

Learners decompose a play into roles, space and options, spot patterns and abstract the key variables, then design and test a simple algorithm (“if X then Y”)—iterating with light data to improve.

How it Works

1Board

Learn strategies and roles (player/coach/umpire) using a fast, collaborative board game.

2Pitch

Move to small-sided games where the same strategies and rules apply in active play.

3Reflect

Use quick prompts or checklists to notice improvements and plan the next round.
